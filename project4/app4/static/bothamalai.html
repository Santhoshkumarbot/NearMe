BOTHAMALAI


Bodhamalai, also known as Bodamalai, is a picturesque hill located in the Eastern Ghats of Tamil Nadu, straddling the Salem and Namakkal districts. The hill rises to an elevation of approximately 1,100 meters and extends over 180 square kilometers, with its highest point reaching 1,200 meters above sea level.It serves as a biodiversity hotspot, featuring dense forests and a variety of flora and fauna. The hill is accessible via off-trail trekking from Salem, offering a challenging yet rewarding experience for hikers .
